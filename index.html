Zanzibar Tours & Excursions

Zanzibar is more than an island — its a paradise full of history, spice, and adventure. Discover our handpicked tours designed to show you the best of the island.

Island Excursions

Stone Town Tour

Explore Zanzibar’s historic heart, UNESCO World Heritage Site, rich with culture and architecture.

Prison Island Tour

Short boat trip to this historic island to see giant tortoises, enjoy beach time, and learn the islands colonial history.

Spice Tour

Discover Zanzibars aromatic farms and taste fresh spices and tropical fruits.

Jozani Forest Tour

Meet the rare Red Colobus monkeys and enjoy a nature walk.

Beach & Relaxation

Nungwi & Kendwa Beach Day

White sands, turquoise sea, and beach vibes.

The Rock Restaurant Visit

Sandbank Picnic (Nakupenda)

Zanzibar Hidden Gems

Discover secret beaches, off-the-beaten-path locations, and authentic island experiences that most tourists never find.

Paje Secret Beach

A secluded stretch of pristine white sand away from crowds, perfect for peaceful relaxation and stunning sunset views.

Kuza Cave Swimming

Swim in a natural limestone cave with crystal-clear freshwater, surrounded by ancient rock formations and local culture.

Local Village Experience

Visit authentic Zanzibari villages, meet local artisans, and experience daily life away from tourist areas.

Fumba Peninsula Sunset

Experience breathtaking sunsets from the quiet Fumba Peninsula, with views of nearby islands and traditional dhows.

Michamvi Beach

A tranquil, less-developed beach on the southeast coast with authentic local restaurants and incredible snorkeling.

Makunduchi Traditional Fishing Village

Witness traditional fishing methods, explore local markets, and experience authentic coastal Swahili culture.

Mnemba Island Snorkeling

Snorkel around exclusive Mnemba Atoll, home to vibrant coral reefs, tropical fish, and sea turtles.

Mtoni Palace Ruins

Explore the atmospheric ruins of a 19th-century sultan's palace with fascinating history and ocean views.

Nungwi Night Market

Experience local life at the vibrant night market with street food, crafts, and authentic Zanzibari atmosphere.
