About This Destination

The Serengeti National Park is Tanzania's most famous wildlife sanctuary and one of Africa's most spectacular destinations. Covering 14,750 square kilometers of pristine wilderness, the park is home to the world's largest animal migration, where over 2 million wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles traverse the plains in search of fresh grazing.

Beyond the Great Migration, the Serengeti offers year-round exceptional wildlife viewing opportunities. The park is home to all members of the Big Five - lions, leopards, elephants, buffalo, and rhinoceros - along with cheetahs, hyenas, wild dogs, and over 500 bird species.

Our carefully crafted safari experience takes you to the heart of this magnificent ecosystem, where you'll witness nature's greatest spectacle while staying in comfortable accommodations and enjoying expert guidance from our professional safari guides.
